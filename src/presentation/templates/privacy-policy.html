Policy overview

Introduction

Attendrix is an enterprise attendance management SaaS platform that helps institutions automate attendance, reporting, and compliance workflows. We built this policy to explain clearly how we collect, protect, and use data across demo onboarding and platform access.

Privacy is a product principle at Attendrix: we limit what we collect, secure what we use, and keep every customer conversation transparent and accountable. This policy applies to all visitors, demo requesters, and platform users.

Data collection

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide, secure, and improve our platform. Here is what we collect and why:

User information

Name, email address, phone number, job role, and login preferences. Used to personalize demo requests, communicate effectively, and set up accounts.

Institution details

Organization name, size, industry type, and operational context. Helps us tailor attendance workflows and understand your environment.

Demo & booking data

Preferred meeting times, timezone, scheduling notes, demo objectives, and follow-up preferences. Used to coordinate demonstrations.

Analytics data

Page views, feature interactions, session durations, and workflow completion rates. Used to improve product clarity and performance.

System usage data

IP address, browser type and version, device type, operating system, and server logs. Collected to protect platform stability and detect abuse.

Usage

How We Use Your Data

Onboarding & setup

We use your information to process demo requests, verify your institution, schedule meetings, and accelerate account setup.

Analytics & improvement

Product usage and performance data help us refine workflows, prioritize features, and deliver a better enterprise experience.

Scheduling & communication

Booking details are used to coordinate meetings, send confirmations, reminders, and deliver the right demo agenda.

Platform quality

We analyze feedback, error reports, and usage trends to improve security, reliability, performance, and user outcomes.

Security monitoring

System logs and anomaly detection help us identify and respond to unauthorized access, fraud attempts, and abuse patterns.

The data we collect is never sold to third parties for advertising. Your information is used exclusively to support our service, protect your account, and maintain compliance.

Protection

Security & Protection

Attendrix employs a defense-in-depth security strategy. Every layer of our infrastructure is designed to protect your data:

TLS 1.2+ in transit AES-256 at rest RBAC & least privilege SOC 2-ready ISO 27001-aligned
  • Encryption in transit & at rest: We protect data with TLS 1.2+ for all network communications and AES-256-level encryption for stored data across all layers.
  • Secure infrastructure: Cloud-hosted architecture designed for SOC 2 readiness and aligned with ISO 27001 control frameworks. All infrastructure is monitored 24/7.
  • Access control: Role-based permissions (RBAC) with a least-privilege model ensure only authorized personnel can access sensitive information.
  • Anti-abuse systems: Rate limiting, anomaly detection, behavioral analysis, and threat monitoring prevent fraud and abusive behavior.
  • Database protection: Encrypted snapshots, secure off-site backups, and automated integrity checks preserve data availability and durability.
  • Monitoring: Comprehensive audit logging, intrusion detection, and real-time alerting support rapid incident response and operational transparency.

Rights & control

User Rights & Control

Attendrix gives users clear control over personal data. We support data subject rights in line with GDPR (Articles 15–22) and CCPA (Civil Code sections 1798.100–1798.199).

Right to access

Request a copy of your personal data and receive a clear, machine-readable explanation of how it is processed.

Right to correction

Update inaccurate or outdated information through platform settings or by contacting our privacy team.

Right to deletion

Request removal of personal data, subject to regulatory retention requirements and operational necessities.

Platform controls

Manage notifications, consent preferences, and data export directly from your account dashboard.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@attendrix.com or use the form below. We acknowledge all requests within 24 hours and resolve them within 30 days.

Retention

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our service and meet legal obligations. Our retention schedules are reviewed quarterly.

Demo requests

Stored for up to 2 years to enable follow-up, historical reference, and compliance auditing.

Onboarding records

Retained for the full lifecycle of the account plus 90 days following closure, then archived securely.

Security logs

Retained for at least 1 year to support incident response, forensic analysis, and audit reporting.

Deletion practice

Soft-delete enables recovery for 30 days. Hard-delete permanently removes data. Backup snapshots are retained under controlled windows.
