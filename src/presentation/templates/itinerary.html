Conference Schedule and Activities

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM
Registration
Participants arrive and complete registration process. Name tags and conference materials will be distributed.
10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Opening Ceremony
Welcome address and keynote speeches from distinguished guests and institution leadership.
11:00 AM - 11:30 AM
Coffee Break
Networking session with refreshments served in the main lobby area.
11:30 AM - 1:00 PM
Workshop Sessions
Parallel workshop sessions covering various topics. Participants can choose sessions based on their interests and expertise levels.
1:00 PM - 2:00 PM
Lunch
Buffet lunch served in the dining hall. Special dietary options available upon request.
2:00 PM - 3:30 PM
Panel Discussion
Interactive panel discussion with industry experts on current trends and future directions in educational technology.
3:30 PM - 4:00 PM
Afternoon Break
Short break with refreshments and networking opportunities.
4:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Closing Ceremony
Summary of the day's events, awards presentation, and closing remarks.
